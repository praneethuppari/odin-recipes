Lemon Pepper Wings

Description

These are wings that are baked in the oven that are flavored with lemon pepper seasoning.

Ingredients

  • 3 lb chicken wings, separated into wingettes and drumettes
  • 3 tbsp extra light olive oil or avocado oil
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 tbsp lemon-pepper seasoning

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425Â°F. Place the chicken wings into a large bowl and drizzle them with oil.
  2. Place wings on a baking sheet, leaving space between each one. Bake the chicken wings for 40 minutes or until they turn golden brown and crispy.
  3. Melt the butter in a medium size bowl and stir in lemon pepper seasoning. Add the wings to the butter mixture and toss until the wings are evenly coated in the seasoning. Serve while the wings are still warm. Enjoy!
